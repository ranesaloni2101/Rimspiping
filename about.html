About Us

Who We Are

Our company is the result of a powerful collaboration between two well-established firms with rich industry heritage, one founded in 1994 and the other in 2003. With over two decades of combined expertise in piping installation and tank fabrication, we bring unmatched experience, precision, and dedication to every project we undertake.

As one of the fastest-growing names in the fabrication and service industry, we specialize in the supply and installation of premium-quality plastic and thermoplastic piping systems. Our offerings include PP, PPH, and HDPE chemical storage tanks, piping grids, and a wide range of installation services tailored to various industrial needs.

We are committed to delivering industry-compliant, reliable, and high-performance solutions. Our product range features cutting-edge jointing technologies such as Socket Fusion, Butt Fusion, Electro-Fusion, IR Fusion, and BCF – ensuring long-lasting, leak-proof connections and system integrity.


With a robust workforce of over 110 professionals – including engineers, managers, CAD designers, and skilled technicians – we bring a combined experience of more than 800 years to the table. Our certified welders and experienced tradespeople have been instrumental in planning, designing, supplying, and commissioning piping systems for diverse process and electromechanical industries, both domestically and internationally.

From a humble beginning over 13 years ago, our company has steadily evolved into a leading provider of non-metallic piping solutions. Our growth is driven by a deep understanding of market dynamics, a strong focus on quality, and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

At our core, we believe in building long-term partnerships through excellence, trust, and innovation.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, innovative, and sustainable plastic piping solutions that empower industries to operate safely and efficiently. We are committed to setting new standards through advanced technology and excellent service.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted partner in industrial piping by setting benchmarks in quality, service, and environmental responsibility.
