About Us

Who We Are

Built on the legacy of two established firms from 1994 and 2003, our company brings together over two decades of expertise in piping installation and tank fabrication. With a 110+ strong team and 800+ years of combined experience, we specialize in delivering reliable non-metallic piping solutions using advanced jointing methods like Socket Fusion, Butt Fusion, and Electro-Fusion. From modest beginnings to global reach, our growth is fueled by precision, quality, and a commitment to long-term partnerships.
